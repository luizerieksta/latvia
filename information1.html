Latvia was established in 1918. In 2018 the country is celebrating its 100 years. The first 100 years haven’t always been easy for Latvians. The country was occupied by the Soviet Union for almost half a decade as well as Germany for a few years. Latvians regained their independence in 1990 and had to fight to keep it. These events have left an impact on the country’s demographics so let’s have a look!
